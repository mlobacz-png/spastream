## 1. INTRODUCTION AND SCOPE

**1.1 Purpose**

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the SpaStream platform and services ("Platform"). This AUP is designed to protect SpaStream, our users, and the integrity of our services.

**1.2 Agreement to Policy**

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to comply with this AUP. This AUP is incorporated into and forms part of the Master Service Agreement ("MSA") between you and SpaStream.

**1.3 Who This Applies To**

This AUP applies to:
- All clients and subscribers ("Client")
- All users, including providers, staff, and administrators ("Users")
- Any person or entity accessing the Platform

**1.4 Binding Effect**

Clients are responsible for ensuring that all Users comply with this AUP. Violations by any User may result in suspension or termination of Client's account.

## 2. PERMITTED USES

**2.1 Authorized Business Purposes**

The Platform may be used only for:

a) **Medical Spa Operations**: Managing legitimate medical spa, aesthetic clinic, or wellness practice operations

b) **Patient/Client Care**: Providing healthcare and aesthetic services to clients/patients

c) **Business Administration**: Managing appointments, billing, communications, and other business functions

d) **Professional Communications**: Communicating with clients/patients regarding their care and services

e) **Marketing**: Marketing services to existing and prospective clients (in compliance with applicable law and HIPAA)

**2.2 Professional Standards**

Use of the Platform must:
- Comply with all applicable medical and professional regulations
- Adhere to ethical standards for healthcare providers
- Respect patient privacy and confidentiality
- Maintain professional conduct in all communications

**2.3 Authorized Users**

Only individuals authorized by Client may access the Platform:
- Each User must have unique credentials
- Credentials may not be shared between individuals
- Access must be limited to personnel with legitimate business need
- Terminated employees' access must be promptly disabled

## 3. PROHIBITED USES

**3.1 Illegal Activities**

You may NOT use the Platform to:

a) **Violate Laws**: Violate any federal, state, local, or international law or regulation

b) **Fraud**: Engage in any fraudulent, deceptive, or misleading practices

c) **Unauthorized Practice**: Practice medicine or provide healthcare services without proper licensing or credentials

d) **Controlled Substances**: Illegally prescribe, distribute, or market controlled substances or drugs

e) **Insurance Fraud**: Submit false claims to insurance companies or government healthcare programs

f) **Identity Theft**: Steal or misuse personal information of clients, patients, or others

g) **Money Laundering**: Use the Platform to launder money or conduct illegal financial transactions

**3.2 Abuse and Harassment**

You may NOT use the Platform to:

a) **Harassment**: Harass, threaten, stalk, or intimidate any person

b) **Hate Speech**: Transmit hate speech, discriminatory content, or content that promotes violence

c) **Bullying**: Engage in cyberbullying or abusive behavior toward clients, patients, staff, or others

d) **Sexual Content**: Send unsolicited sexual or explicit content to clients or others

e) **Defamation**: Publish false or defamatory statements about individuals or entities

**3.3 Privacy and Security Violations**

You may NOT:

a) **Unauthorized Access**: Access accounts, data, or systems without authorization

b) **Credential Sharing**: Share login credentials or allow unauthorized persons to access your account

c) **Data Theft**: Steal, harvest, or misappropriate personal information or PHI

d) **HIPAA Violations**: Use or disclose PHI in violation of HIPAA or the Data Processing Agreement

e) **Privacy Violations**: Violate patient privacy rights or disclose confidential information without authorization

f) **Surveillance**: Use the Platform to conduct unauthorized surveillance or monitoring

**3.4 Spam and Unsolicited Communications**

You may NOT use the Platform to:

a) **Spam**: Send unsolicited bulk emails, SMS, or other communications ("spam")

b) **Marketing Without Consent**: Send marketing communications without proper consent

c) **Phishing**: Send phishing emails or fraudulent communications impersonating others

d) **Misleading Content**: Send communications with misleading subject lines, sender information, or content

e) **Excessive Volume**: Send excessive communications that burden systems or recipients

f) **Third-Party Marketing**: Use client/patient data for third-party marketing purposes without consent

**3.5 Malicious Activity**

You may NOT:

a) **Malware**: Upload, transmit, or distribute viruses, malware, ransomware, or other malicious code

b) **Hacking**: Attempt to hack, penetrate, or test the security of the Platform or other systems

c) **DDoS Attacks**: Conduct denial-of-service attacks or interfere with service availability

d) **Data Scraping**: Use automated tools (bots, spiders, scrapers) to extract data from the Platform (except authorized API access)

e) **System Abuse**: Overload, disrupt, or degrade the performance of the Platform

f) **Circumvention**: Bypass or circumvent security measures, access controls, or usage limits

**3.6 Intellectual Property Violations**

You may NOT:

a) **Infringement**: Upload or transmit content that infringes copyrights, trademarks, patents, or other intellectual property rights

b) **Piracy**: Use the Platform to distribute pirated software, media, or other content

c) **Reverse Engineering**: Reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or attempt to derive source code from the Platform

d) **Trademark Misuse**: Use SpaStream trademarks, logos, or branding without written permission

e) **Copying**: Copy, reproduce, or create derivative works of the Platform software or documentation

**3.7 Inappropriate Content**

You may NOT upload or transmit:

a) **Illegal Content**: Content that is illegal, obscene, or violates laws

b) **Graphic Violence**: Excessively violent or graphic content (except as medically necessary for treatment documentation)

c) **Exploitation**: Content depicting exploitation or abuse of children or vulnerable individuals

d) **Terrorism**: Content promoting terrorism or violent extremism

e) **False Information**: Deliberately false medical information that could harm patients

**3.8 Commercial Restrictions**

You may NOT:

a) **Resale**: Resell, sublicense, rent, lease, or lend access to the Platform

b) **Competitive Use**: Use the Platform for competitive analysis, benchmarking, or to build competing products

c) **Unauthorized Integration**: Integrate the Platform with unauthorized third-party services or products

d) **Data Mining**: Mine Platform data for commercial purposes beyond Client's own business operations

**3.9 Misrepresentation**

You may NOT:

a) **False Credentials**: Misrepresent your credentials, licenses, or qualifications

b) **Impersonation**: Impersonate another person, business, or entity

c) **False Information**: Provide false information during registration or use

d) **Fake Reviews**: Post fake reviews or testimonials

e) **Unauthorized Claims**: Make unauthorized claims about the Platform's capabilities or endorsements

**3.10 Regulatory and Ethical Violations**

You may NOT:

a) **HIPAA Violations**: Violate HIPAA privacy or security rules

b) **Medical Board Violations**: Violate medical board regulations or professional conduct standards

c) **False Advertising**: Engage in false or misleading advertising of services

d) **Kickbacks**: Participate in illegal kickback arrangements or patient referral schemes

e) **Price Fixing**: Engage in price fixing or anti-competitive practices

You must promptly report:

c) **Lost Devices**: Lost or stolen devices containing Platform credentials

d) **Suspicious Activity**: Any suspicious activity involving your account

Devices accessing the Platform must:

## 6. COMMUNICATION STANDARDS

**6.1 Professional Communications**

All communications through the Platform must:

a) Be professional and courteous

b) Comply with applicable laws and regulations

c) Respect patient privacy and confidentiality

d) Be accurate and not misleading

e) Include required disclosures and opt-out mechanisms

**6.2 SMS and Email Requirements**

When using SMS and email features, you must:

a) **Obtain Consent**: Obtain prior express written consent for marketing communications

b) **Include Opt-Out**: Provide clear opt-out mechanisms (e.g., "Reply STOP to unsubscribe")

c) **Honor Opt-Outs**: Process opt-out requests immediately and maintain suppression lists

d) **Identify Sender**: Clearly identify yourself as the sender

e) **Comply with TCPA**: Comply with Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) requirements

f) **Comply with CAN-SPAM**: Comply with CAN-SPAM Act for email communications

**6.3 Prohibited Communications**

You may NOT send:

a) Robocalls or automated voice messages without consent

b) Marketing messages to numbers on the National Do Not Call Registry (without established business relationship)

c) Messages outside permitted hours (8am-9pm local time for marketing)

d) Deceptive or misleading communications

e) Communications after opt-out request

**6.4 Medical Advice Limitations**

When communicating through the Platform:

a) Do not provide medical advice without proper patient-provider relationship

b) Clearly indicate when communications are educational vs. medical advice

c) Direct patients to seek in-person care when appropriate

d) Maintain appropriate boundaries and professional conduct

## 11. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATIONS

**11.1 Enforcement Actions**

If you violate this AUP, SpaStream may:

a) **Warning**: Issue a written warning and require corrective action

b) **Suspension**: Temporarily suspend access to your account or specific features

c) **Termination**: Immediately terminate your account and the MSA

d) **Data Retention**: Retain data as necessary for investigation or legal proceedings

e) **Legal Action**: Pursue legal remedies, including injunctive relief and damages

f) **Cooperation**: Cooperate with law enforcement and regulatory authorities

**11.2 Factors Considered**

In determining appropriate enforcement action, we consider:

a) Severity of the violation

b) Impact on other users or systems

c) Whether violation was intentional or negligent

d) History of prior violations

e) Willingness to cooperate and remediate

f) Legal or regulatory requirements

**11.3 Immediate Termination**

SpaStream may immediately terminate your account without prior notice for:

a) Illegal activity

b) Serious security violations or threats

c) HIPAA violations

d) Fraud or financial crimes

e) Repeated violations after warnings

f) Activities causing harm to SpaStream, users, or third parties

**11.4 No Refunds**

Termination for AUP violations does not entitle you to refund of any fees.

**11.5 Data Access Upon Termination**

If your account is terminated for AUP violations:

a) You will have 7 days (not 30) to export your data

b) SpaStream may retain data longer for investigation or legal purposes

c) SpaStream may refuse to return data that contains evidence of violations or illegal activity
