2. **"$200,000+ annual value"** (ROI)
3. **"Actually HIPAA compliant"** (fear/safety)
4. **"Pays for itself 28x over"** (no-brainer math)
5. **"Built for medical spas, not gyms"** (positioning)

---

## Visual Cues & Energy

- **Pace**: Fast but not rushed. Confidence, not desperation.
- **Transitions**: Smooth. Practice click paths so there's no fumbling.
- **Energy**: Enthusiasm about AI features, concern about compliance, confidence on pricing.
- **Hand gestures**: Point at key metrics, circle important numbers.
- **Pauses**: After big numbers ($200K, 32%, 40%) let them sink in for 2 seconds.

---

## What to SKIP in 5-Minute Version

- Inventory management (unless specifically requested)
- Staff management details
- Marketing automation deep dive
- SMS feature walkthrough
- Detailed package creation
- Invoice generation process

**Why skip these?** They're "nice to have" features every platform has. The demo should focus on DIFFERENTIATION (AI) and URGENCY (lost revenue, compliance risk).

→ "Mindbody is solid for basic scheduling. But are you happy losing $40K/year to no-shows? Are you comfortable that they're not HIPAA compliant? The question is: happy enough to leave $200K on the table?"

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Absolutely. While you're thinking, can I ask: what would need to be true for this to be a no-brainer? [Listen] Great, let me address that specifically..."

**"Too expensive"**
→ "I hear that. Let's do the math: $599/month = $7,188/year. Our average client makes an ADDITIONAL $150K in revenue and saves $50K in costs. You're not buying software—you're investing $7K to make $200K. Would you make that trade?"

**"We just signed a 2-year contract with [competitor]"**
→ "Ouch. How much is the cancellation fee? Even if it's $5,000, you'll make that back in the first month with our no-show predictor. Early cancellation becomes a strategic investment, not a sunk cost."

## Success Metrics for Demo

A successful demo results in:
1. Trial signup OR sales call booked
2. Prospect saying "I need to see the AI features in my practice"
3. Price objection overcome with ROI math
4. Urgency created around lost revenue/compliance risk

**Red flag if:** Prospect says "Let me think about it" without specific concerns. Push for clarity: "What specifically would you like to think about? Maybe I can help."

---

## Final Pro Tips

1. **Record yourself** doing the demo 3x before going live. Watch for "ums," awkward pauses, fumbling.
2. **Time each section** with a stopwatch. If you're at 3:30 and haven't hit AI features, you're too slow.
3. **Have 2-3 versions ready**: 5-minute executive summary, 15-minute full demo, 30-minute technical deep-dive.
4. **Always end 30 seconds early**. Leave them wanting more, not checking their watch.
5. **The best demo script is the one you don't read from**. Internalize these beats, then speak naturally.

**Remember**: You're not selling software. You're showing them a $200,000 opportunity they're currently leaving on the table. Act like it.
