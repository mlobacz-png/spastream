## Critical Success Factors

**1. Consistency**
- Post on social media EVERY day
- Send cold emails EVERY day
- Conduct demos EVERY week
- Engage with target audience EVERY day

**2. Quality Over Quantity**
- Better to have 10 happy customers than 50 frustrated ones
- Focus on creating real value
- Over-deliver on promises

**3. Feedback Loop**
- Listen to every customer
- Implement quick wins immediately
- Communicate changes and updates
- Build in public

**4. Relationships First**
- Focus on helping, not selling
- Become a trusted advisor
- Add value before asking for anything
- Build genuine connections

**5. Resilience**
- Expect rejection and setbacks
- Learn from every "no"
- Celebrate small wins
- Stay focused on the vision
