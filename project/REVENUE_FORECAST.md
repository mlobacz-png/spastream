# SpaStream Revenue Forecast & Market Analysis

## Market Overview

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)

**US Medical Spa Market:**
- Total medical spas in the US: ~7,500 - 8,000
- Annual market growth rate: 15-20%
- Industry revenue: $15.5 billion (2024)
- Projected to reach $32 billion by 2030

**Target Customer Profile:**
- Small to medium med spas (1-5 locations): ~6,000 businesses
- Multi-location chains (6-20 locations): ~400 businesses
- Enterprise chains (20+ locations): ~100 businesses

**Primary Target:** Small to medium practices (6,000 businesses)

## Pricing Strategy

### Premium Pricing Model (Value-Based)

**Tier 1 - Starter:** $299/month
- 1-2 providers
- Unlimited clients
- All core features included
- Email support
- Target: Solo practitioners, small studios
- Market share: 20% of customers

**Tier 2 - Professional:** $599/month
- 3-7 providers
- Unlimited clients
- All features + AI recommendations
- Priority email support
- Advanced analytics
- Target: Most med spas (primary market)
- Market share: 50% of customers

**Tier 3 - Premium:** $999/month
- 8-15 providers
- Multiple locations
- All features + AI optimization
- Priority phone/chat support
- Custom reporting
- Dedicated success manager (quarterly check-ins)
- Target: Growing multi-location practices
- Market share: 20% of customers

**Tier 4 - Enterprise:** $1,499/month
- Unlimited providers
- Unlimited locations
- White-label options
- 24/7 priority support
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations & API access
- Target: Large chains and franchises
- Market share: 10% of customers

**Average Revenue Per Account (ARPA):** $699/month ($8,388/year)

**Tier Distribution Assumptions:**
- Starter (20%): $299
- Professional (50%): $599
- Premium (20%): $999
- Enterprise (10%): $1,499
- Weighted Average: (0.20 × $299) + (0.50 × $599) + (0.20 × $999) + (0.10 × $1,499) = $699/month

### Additional Revenue Streams

**Payment Processing Platform Fee:** 2% platform fee via Stripe Connect (on volume over $50k/month)
- Industry average transaction: $600
- Average monthly processing by tier:
  - Starter tier: $30,000/month → Platform fee: $0/month (below threshold)
  - Professional tier: $56,000/month → Platform fee: $120/month (2% × $6k excess)
  - Premium tier: $80,000/month → Platform fee: $600/month (2% × $30k excess)
  - Enterprise tier: $120,000/month → Platform fee: $1,400/month (2% × $70k excess)
- Weighted average platform fee per customer: ~$344/month

**SMS Credits:** $0.02 per message (through Twilio)
- Average practice sends 500 SMS/month
- Monthly revenue: $10/practice
- High-volume practices: 2,000+ SMS/month = $40+

**Professional Services:**
- Data migration: $500-$2,000 one-time
- Custom integrations: $2,000-$10,000
- Training sessions: $200/hour
- Estimated: $1,000 per customer (first year average)

- Boulevard: Pretty but limited features
- Vagaro: Budget option, missing advanced features

- Purpose-built for med spas (not generic booking)
- Modern, beautiful UX that drives adoption
- Comprehensive feature set eliminates need for multiple tools

**3. High Switching Costs (once acquired):**
- Customer data lock-in (in a good way - they don't want to migrate)
- Staff training investment
- Integration with existing workflows
- Client portal adoption

**4. Network Effects:**
- Reviews and recommendations between med spa owners
- Industry associations and conferences
- Client expectations (clients who use portal at one spa expect it elsewhere)

**5. Market Fragmentation:**
- No dominant player with >30% market share currently
- Room for a category leader to emerge
- Med spa owners actively seeking better solutions
