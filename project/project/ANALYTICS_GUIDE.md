## Key Metrics Explained

### Financial Metrics

**Total Revenue**
- Sum of all completed transactions
- Includes all payment types
- Excludes refunds

**Revenue Growth**
- Comparison to previous period
- Formula: ((Current - Previous) / Previous) × 100
- Positive = growth, Negative = decline

**Average Transaction Value**
- Total revenue ÷ number of transactions
- Indicates typical sale size
- Higher = better upselling

### Client Metrics

**Client Lifetime Value (CLV)**
```
CLV = Total All-Time Revenue / Total Clients
```
- Higher CLV = more valuable clients
- Increases with retention
- Goal: Maximize through packages/memberships

**Retention Rate**
```
Retention Rate = (Returning Clients / Total Clients) × 100
```
- % of clients who book again
- Industry average: 40-60%
- Good: 60-75%, Excellent: 75%+

**Churn Rate**
```
Churn Rate = (Inactive Clients / Total Clients) × 100
```
- % of clients who stop coming
- Inactive = no appointments in 90 days
- Industry average: 20-40%
- Goal: Under 30%

### Operational Metrics

**Completion Rate**
```
Completion Rate = (Completed / Total Appointments) × 100
```
- % of appointments that happen
- Industry average: 70-85%
- Goal: Above 80%

**No-Show Rate**
```
No-Show Rate = (No-Shows / Total Appointments) × 100
```
- % of missed appointments
- Industry average: 10-20%
- Goal: Under 10%

**Cancellation Rate**
```
Cancellation Rate = (Cancelled / Total Appointments) × 100
```
- % of cancelled appointments
- Acceptable: 5-15%
- Goal: Under 10%

## Interpreting Your Data

### Revenue Insights

**Growing Revenue**
If revenue is increasing:
✅ Continue current marketing efforts
✅ Invest in staff training
✅ Expand popular services
✅ Consider raising prices gradually

**Declining Revenue**
If revenue is decreasing:
⚠️ Review pricing strategy
⚠️ Increase marketing spend
⚠️ Launch promotions
⚠️ Improve customer service
⚠️ Check competition

**Seasonal Patterns**
- Q4 (Oct-Dec): Typically highest (holidays)
- Q1 (Jan-Mar): New Year resolutions boost
- Q2 (Apr-Jun): Wedding season spike
- Q3 (Jul-Sep): Summer vacation dip

### Client Insights

**High Lifetime Value**
- Clients are loyal and spend well
- Focus on retention programs
- Create VIP tiers
- Request referrals

**Low Lifetime Value**
- Clients only visit once or twice
- Improve first-time experience
- Offer package deals
- Follow up after visits
- Implement loyalty rewards

**High Churn Rate**
Possible reasons:
- Poor customer service
- Pricing too high
- Better competitors nearby
- Inconsistent results
- Lack of follow-up

Solutions:
- Survey lost clients
- Improve communication
- Offer win-back promotions
- Enhance treatment quality
- Implement loyalty programs

### Appointment Insights

**High No-Show Rate**
Causes:
- No reminder system
- Too much advance booking time
- Easy to cancel/reschedule
- No deposit required

Solutions:
- Implement SMS reminders
- Require deposits
- Tighter cancellation policy
- Reduce booking window
- Confirm day before

**Low Completion Rate**
Issues:
- Poor scheduling
- Long wait times
- Staff shortages
- Overbooking

Solutions:
- Use AI no-show predictor
- Buffer time between appointments
- Hire additional staff
- Improve time management

## Common Questions

**Q: Why is my revenue by service not matching invoices?**
A: Analytics only counts completed transactions. Check that payments are marked as "completed."

**Q: How is lifetime value calculated?**
A: Total all-time revenue divided by total number of clients (not just period).

**Q: What counts as a "churned" client?**
A: Any client with no appointments in the last 90 days.

**Q: Can I see analytics for specific staff members?**
A: Staff performance analytics coming soon! Track revenue and appointments per provider.

**Q: How often does data update?**
A: Real-time! Analytics update as soon as you record transactions or complete appointments.

**Q: Can I compare different time periods?**
A: Currently shows growth vs. previous equal period. Custom comparisons coming soon.

**Q: Why don't I see historical data?**
A: Analytics show data from when you started using the system. Historical data before that isn't included.

**Q: Can I export this data?**
A: Export feature coming soon. You'll be able to download PDF and CSV reports.
