- Monthly payments of $60,000 = $200 platform fee (2% × $10,000 excess)
- Monthly payments of $80,000 = $600 platform fee (2% × $30,000 excess)
- Monthly payments of $100,000 = $1,000 platform fee (2% × $50,000 excess)
- Monthly payments of $150,000 = $2,000 platform fee (2% × $100,000 excess)

**Competitive Comparison:**

- $100,000/month volume: Save $1,900/month ($22,800/year) vs. competitors
