# SpaStream Pricing Strategy & Value Justification

## Executive Summary

SpaStream uses value-based pricing that reflects the substantial ROI we deliver to medical spas. Our pricing strategy positions us as a premium solution while remaining highly accessible given the proven returns.

**Key Pricing Philosophy:**
- Price based on value delivered, not cost to produce
- Each tier pays for itself within the first month through efficiency gains
- Premium positioning attracts higher-quality, lower-churn customers
- Transparent pricing with no hidden fees (except optional add-ons)

**2. Attracts Right Customers:**

### Price Anchoring Strategy

**Tier 4 (Enterprise) at $1,499 makes:**
- Tier 3 (Premium) at $999 seem reasonable
- Tier 2 (Professional) at $599 seem like a great deal
- Tier 1 (Starter) at $299 seem like a steal

**Most customers choose:** Tier 2 (Professional) at $599

- Includes AI features (key differentiator)
- Feels like they're getting premium value at mid-tier price

"Those are exactly the problems we solved. Plus, we're the only platform with AI-powered features like treatment recommendations and no-show prediction. Want to see a demo of how those work?"

Think about it: if you save just 5 hours per week on scheduling and admin, that's 5 more hours for seeing patients. At $200/hour, that's $4,000/month in additional revenue capacity.
